On a Journey


Hello World, I am SumOne.


I am just like you. In fact in what Ive seen of the world we all share allot in common. We are all humans, seeking survival, happiness, friendship comfort and our place here on this world of ours. I am just like you, I am confused, ignorant, scared, and for the most part acting out of instinct and habbit rather than acting consciously. I am offended by the suffering which is all around and allowed to continue every day. I am lost in the chaos of the world, and in the impossible task of finding and understanding the truth of myself and my surroundings.


Yet...

I love my planet and the vibrant life here. The art and culture that people create and share with one another is exquisit. The beauty and the kindness I see from so many people, combined with the miracle of the Earth and the abundance it provides, leaves me feeling blessed everyday. Every breath I take, every glass of water I drink, every meal and their many ingrediants I eat... Ifeel so fortunate in this life that I say "Thank You", but thats not enough.


I am SumOne, someone who has decided to live life pursuing the good of the whole. I follow my curiosity in the hopes that there may yet be truth to be found... hidden in the chaos and information of the world.



I am SumOne, I aim to walk, talk, ask and learn... help others, build, connect and trust in others as they have trusted in me. I dedicate my life to the journey of studying and working, every moment, in the hopes that hummanity and the greater picture may find some value in understanding the Self, the World, and the Humanity we are all a part of.


We all feel a potential to be something great, and the power to change the world. We all feel the fabric of life which connects us to one another and to the world at large. We, humans, are amazing creatures, we are powerful creatures, look at what we can do when we work together. Its time we start using that power, our power, responsibly, and purposefully. Take your personal power back, and start creating the world you have always wanted to see.
