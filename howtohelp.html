How To Help

Helping comes in many shapes and forms. Being part of the web of hummanity gives you two major options in how to affect the world. You can turn your attention to the world within, your own consious parradigm, where ultimately you have the most influence. Or you can turn your focus outwards, to the exterior world, where there are many ways to help others. Because we are all linked, both serve the good of the whole, and the good of the self.


Help Yourself

The most necessary and difficult of tasks. It is often challenging to listen to ourselves and to know what it is what we need. It is even more challenging to apply this knowledge to our daily routine. Helping yourself is a multi faceted job. It requires taking care of your physical body, taking care of your emotional/mental self, taking care of your connection to the whole, your spiritual self, and finally taking care of your relationships, others. Ultimately these all intertwine, and when we are lacking in one, it takes tolls on the other facets of being.


Help Your Community

Reach out into your personal sphere of influence. We have the power to make great changes locally if we put our minds to it. Just passing one smile to one person on your commute changes the world. Talk to people, get involved, find out what people need to live better lives and facilitate it through conversation and work.


Help Our Jounrey

The main way you can help us personally, is by sharing human connections. Are you on our projected path? Do you have friends and family where we will be walking? Connect us! You can even use the map app(globe in top right corner) and drop a marker on the map along with whatever information you'd like. Just click add shelter and follow the form. The more people who participate, the less daunting this journey will be.

One of the main points of the walking journey is frugality. We don't carry much and each item we take, serves an important purpose. If it arises that a new item is needed, we will be asking for help in getting what we need. A list will be posted here...
